Nucleus [ˈnju:klɪəs/]

noun: nucleus; plural noun: nuclei

Nucleus was born from our sister company EasyComp which still provides IT solutions for the commercial market. Due to our increase growth in the Audio Visual and Systems integration market within EasyComp, we have now rebranded the Audio Visual arm of EasyComp as Nucleus.

The team members have backgrounds in Design, Networks, Telecoms, Programming, Audio, Video, Electrics, and Construction which all play an important part in a successful installation.
